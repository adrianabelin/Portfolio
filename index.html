About

Hey everyone, my name is Adriana! I'm currently studying at the University of Minnesota for a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Graphic Design. Every since I propped open my first box of colored crayons, I've been hugely interested in the role of color in art and how it sets mood and involks emotional reactions in the veiwer. However, more recently I have been interested in learning how to better impliment function and meaning into my art, and how to strike a balance between form and function. I plan to continue learning more about concept implimentation as I continue onward in my studies.

This portfolio includes pieces made using photoshop, illustrator, my Nikon D7200 camera or acyrillic paint which were done as a class assignment or in my spare time. Some motifs and art styles you may notice in my art includes whimsical themes, flat design, and illustrative work. As a side note, the Mobineo and "TL" logo can be clicked to reveal another image explaining my thought process behind the brand and logo design. I've also illustrated a couple of books for class which are not currently in my portfolio, so it's my intention to add them soon!
